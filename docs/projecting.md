# Projecting slides

## Prepare to project items in the service list:

Before the Mass begins, allow time to review the sections and items in
the service list.

1.  In the top right corner of the **Presenter** window, click the
    **Screens** icon; then click **Off**. This turns off projection to
    the two screens in the nave, but you can see what will be displayed
    in the preview at the bottom left of the **Presenter** window,
    underneath the service list.
2.  Using your mouse or the arrow keys on the keyboard, step through the
    items in the service list to verify that they are all present and
    that they match the order of Mass for the day.

## Project the service list items manually

When you have verified that everything is ready, in the top right corner
of the **Presenter** window, click the **Screens** icon; then click
**On**. This turns on projection to the screens in the nave.

Some items in the service list are single slides, for example, the media
images used to advertize our Parish events. Some comprise a set of two
or more slides, for example, those used for the call and response parts
of the Mass, the Creed, and so on, and those used to display hymns and
songs.

1.  Use the arrow keys to step through and open the items in the service
    and the slides sequentially. You can also use the mouse to select
    items and slides individually.
2.  During the twenty minutes or so before Mass begins, step through the
    media slides at the top of the services list.
3.  Druring Mass, follow the the order of service carefully and try to
    anticipate the next slide.
