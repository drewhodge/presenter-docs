* Services

*Presenter's* main window shows a menu bar, a *Quick Start* bar and a *Recents* list--a list of recently opened services. Services

** To open a service:

   1. Identify the service you want to open; each has a name, a date and title for the day of the liturgical season. For example:
      *Mar 22*
      *5th Sunday of Lent 10am*
   2. If the service is listed in the *Recents* list, click the service's name.
   3. If the service is not listed in the *Recents* list, in the *Quick Start* bar, click *Open Service*; or in the menu bar, click *Services*.
   4. In the list of service, click the one you want to open.
      *Note:*  If you don't see your service listed, click *View All* to expand the list.

*Presenter* displays the service as individual sections, or items, on the left side of the window.
